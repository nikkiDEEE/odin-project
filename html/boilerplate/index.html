Indian Stuff

Indian Cuisine

Biryani

A popular and flavorful rice dish made with aromatic spices, rice, and meat (chicken, mutton, or fish), often garnished with boiled eggs and fried onions.

Masala Dosa

A crispy pancake made from fermented rice and urad dal (lentil) batter, filled with a spicy potato mixture.

Chai (my personal favourite)

Traditional Indian tea made with a blend of black tea, milk, spices (such as cardamom, ginger and black pepper!), and sweetened with sugar.

  2. Uthappam (main course)
